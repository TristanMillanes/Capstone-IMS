Data Privacy & Security Protocols

By requesting credentials to the Provincial Government Environment and Natural Resources Office (PGENRO) Information Management System (IMS), you agree to comply with the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012).

Operational Security Rules:

  • Credential Security: You must not share designated passwords, login tokens, or authentication details.
  • Usage Integrity: Extracted metrics and information are strictly authorized for validated government processes.
  • Active Audits: Processes, file accesses, downloads, and inputs are archived directly to your security index.

Violations are subject to disciplinary steps governed by regional civil service regulations.
